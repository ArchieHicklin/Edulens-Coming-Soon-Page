Connect pricing

When you use Connect with Express or Custom accounts, you can customize the user experience, control payout timing, and more.

Accounts

Applies to active Express or Custom accounts (sellers or recipients) on your platform. An account is considered active in any month that it receives funds from your platform. These charges do not apply when using Connect with Standard accounts.

Includes
  • Onboarding and verification
  • Support for a fully customizable UI
  • Flexible funds routing to support your business model
  • Comprehensive platform-level reporting tools
  • Customizable reporting for accounts
0,5 % of account volume ? Account volume refers to funds sent to an account by your platform

Platforms at scale

Do you expect your platform to process more than 150 000 $US/month? Reach out to discuss volume pricing for large platforms.

Low volume accounts

If your platform includes accounts that regularly receive a small monthly payment volume, contact us to discuss alternative pricing options.
